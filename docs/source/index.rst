Welcome to the Center for Cognitive Neuroscience!
===================================

Please find and contribute helpful information for lab usage here.
Main CCN Website: `CCN Website <https://staglincenterforcogneuro.semel.ucla.edu/>`_

**List of commonly used links:**

- `CCN YouTube channel: <https://www.youtube.com/@uclastaglincenterforcognit4262/videos/>`_ The channel contains very useful videos demonstrating common procedures at CCN--how to prepare a participant for a scan, navigating the XA30 upgraded software, placing voxels for spectroscopy, and so on.
- `SIStat system login: <https://www.sistat.ucla.edu/CCNSAS/ShowCalendar.asp#/>`_ This is your gateway to all activities regarding scan scheduling: booking scans, adding lab personnel, checking certification status, linking funds, uploading IRBs, and so on.
- `Scanner schedule: <https://www.sistat.ucla.edu/CCNSAS/login.asp/>`_ You do not need to log in to SISTAT to view the calendar. It is also bookmarked on the control room iMac.
- **Safety certification (and recertification):** This is done through a combination of BruinLearn and in-person training sessions. The process is documented in the MR Training section. All links and resources necessary can be found on the `BruinLearn <https://bruinlearn.ucla.edu/>`_ course. If you log in to BruinLearn (using your UCLA credentials) and do not see the Staglin Center Safety course, you are not yet registered for it and will need to enroll yourself using the `enrollment link <https://bruinlearn.ucla.edu/enroll/6YM6FH/>`_. Lab managers and admin, please make a note of this link for future lab members that need to be safety certified.

   - The annual recertification is also done through the BruinLearn platform, with no password necessary. You will need to notify the MR Techs as instructed once you have passed. Outside of that, please only email CCN admins if you are experiencing issues enrolling in the course or you have questions with the materials.
   - Walkthrough the Operations Training sessions are always in high demand. If you do not see any sessions posted on the BruinLearn calendar, it means they are all full. DO NOT email CCN asking where the sessions are or when we'll post more. If you have extreme time restrictions to work around, you can contact CCN requesting an accommodation. Otherwise, keep an eye on the BruinLearn calendar for new postings, as we offer these sessions as frequently as we can.
